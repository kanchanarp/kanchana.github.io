About Me

I am a grad student at the University of Utah currently enrolled in the PhD program at the School of Computing advised by Prof. Aditya Bhaskara. I completed my BSc in Engineering from the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka majoring in Computer Science and Engineering. I'm interested in research involving approximation algorithms for problems including online learning problems such as online clustering, online learning with partial observations, as well as problems such as matrix completion, principal component regression, weighted low-rank approximation and etc. Outside of the work, I'm also into hiking and outdoor activities.
